    On this website we are going to make different installation guides for general utility software, in Windows and Ubuntu operating systems.

    The manuals will be made step by step and explaining in the most detailed way possible through images.

    At the beginning of each software there will be a brief description of the program to be installed and in some cases, at the end, there will be a comparison between said or related programs.

    Once inside a page you can go to the table of contents and explicitly select the section of the page you want to see so you don't have to scroll down to that section.

    To return to this page, the home page, you will find a drawing of a house at the bottom of each page that will bring you back here.
